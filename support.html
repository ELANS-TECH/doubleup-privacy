Frequently asked

Is my progress saved?
Yes — your net worth, level, properties, and vault are saved on your device automatically.

Does the game cost money or have ads?
No. Double Up is free with no ads and no in-app purchases.

Is there real-money gambling?
No. Double Up is a score-based card game. There is no wagering of real money and nothing to win or lose outside the game.

How do I report a bug?
Email the address above with your device model and a short description of what happened. Screenshots help.
