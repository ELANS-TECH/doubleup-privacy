Privacy Policy — Double Up: Card Money Battle

Last updated: July 17, 2026

The short version

Double Up does not collect, store, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no ads, no analytics, and no third-party tracking. Everything the game saves stays on your own device.

What we collect

Nothing. Double Up does not ask for your name, email, location, contacts, photos, or any other personal data. We do not use advertising identifiers and we do not track you across apps or websites.

Game data

Your game progress — your net worth, level, properties, vault, and settings — is stored locally on your device only. It is never transmitted to us or to anyone else. If you delete the app, this data is removed with it.

Third parties

Double Up contains no third-party advertising, no analytics services, and no social media integrations. No data is sent to outside companies.

Children's privacy

Double Up does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Because the app collects no data at all, there is nothing to collect from children or adults.

Apple services

The app is distributed through Apple's App Store and TestFlight. Any data those Apple services collect (such as crash reports you choose to share, or download statistics) is governed by Apple's own privacy policy, not ours. We do not receive personal information from Apple.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a new "Last updated" date.
